Scissors
by Zivvy Epstein and Dima Smirnov

An interactive demonstration of the Wallace–Bolyai–Gerwien theorem.

Theorem (Wallace-Bolyai-Gerwien)

Any two simple polygons of equal area can be dissected into a finite number of congruent polygonal pieces.

You:
  1. Click anywhere to start drawing the first polygon. Click back in the orange circle when you are finished
  2. Click anyhwere to start drawing the second polygon. Click back in the orange circle when you are finished
We:
  1. Rescale the polygons so that they are of equal area.
  2. Cut the first polygon into triangles.
  3. Cut and rearrange the triangles into a stack of rectangles.
  4. Cut new rectangles, turn them into triangles, and place them inside the second polygon.
